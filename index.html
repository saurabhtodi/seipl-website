Our Story
A Legacy Woven with Dedication
1979 Year Established

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

In 1979, Mr. Damodar Parshad Todi ventured into the field of hosiery knitted fabric from scratch and established Shree Enterprises (India) Private Limited in Ludhiana — a city renowned as the hosiery capital of India.

His sons Suresh Todi and Ramesh Todi joined the family business, each bringing over 30 years of deep expertise. Through decades of hard work, SEIPL has earned its reputation as a trusted manufacturer of high-quality knitted fabrics.

Situated on Bahadur Ke Road (Dana Mandi) near Jalandhar Bypass — the city's hosiery manufacturing hub — and just 5 km from the railway station, we are strategically positioned for seamless logistics.
